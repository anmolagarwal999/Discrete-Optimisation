Discrete Optimizations

This Site contains the various works, including a Guide and Some Simulations, that We Team R took part in during the course of the AAD CS1.301 Project.

The guides are mainly Divided into the chapters Local Search, Linear Programming and Constraint Programming.

There are also 3 Simulators which are a Travelling Salesman Problem(TSP) visualizer, a problem on Knapsack, and A Weighted Directed graph creator for the Gavel Problem.

What is Discrete Optimization

Discrete Optimization deals with Optimization in applied mathematics and computer science, and deals with problems in which some or all variables are restricted to be Discrete variables , i.e., to assume only a discrete set of values, such as the integers.
